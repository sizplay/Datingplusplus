User Guide


1. Registration

Customers may create an account using the register button at the top right of the first webpage. After inserting all the relevant information, the user may submit your sign up form and begin the journey of dating!


2. Log in

Customers can log in using their email and password. You can input your email and password to sign-in fields, which are located on the top right side of the first page. When you click the log in button, the webpage redirects you to the profile where your personal information is displayed. The profile page is the access point to all the major actions that take place in the website for the user.


3. Profile

When customers are on the profile page, you can view your personal and account information, view likes on the side bar, utilize the search and date (pending date, past date and personalized date suggestion list) function. Additionally, you may also check the most active profiles, most highly rated profile, edit your personal account and sign out using the button on the navigation bar.


4. My profile/ Account page

When customers click the My Profile button, it shows all the information about your profile and you may view the personal information in account page.


5. View likes

When customers click view likes, you can see all the information you’ve clicked the other profile ID and the time you clicked. Also you can see other people profile ID and the time that people clicked that other people clicked you profile.


6. Past, Pending date and Personalized date suggestion list

Upon selecting on the Dates drop down menu, the user will be presented with the past, pending date and personalized date suggestion list; the user will be redirected to a page that displays all the information when it comes to the past, pending date and personalized date suggestion list. On the past date, the user will view the partners’ profile ID, your profile ID, time and location of your meeting. A similar set of results will be printed for the pending dates. On the personalized date suggestion list, it shows profile IDs based on your age and other candidates in the dating site.


7. Most active profile and most highly rated profile

Most active profile is that shows active users based on total number of likes they received, number of dates they went on and last time they logged in to their profile. Most highly rated profile is determined by checking the ratings for all the users and sorting them based on the users ratings.


8. Search

Customers can search other profiles based on location, hair color date range age start and end. You will receive a set of profiles based on your filtering and will be able to view details on those profiles.


9. Edit account

Edit account gives you the option to can edit your personal information. Upon changing any or all data, you may submit it and all the inputted data will be saved.


10. Sign out

When you click the sign out button, you will be logged out and brought back to the first page of the site.
